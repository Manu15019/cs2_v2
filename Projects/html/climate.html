Ein Hod


Ein Hod suffers from climate change. They barely get any rain over there. They combat this by building sustainable arcitecture (i.e. Houses made out of hemp).

These hemp houses are effective, but there aren't many of them. There is one that lays on the main road on the hill. There are a few others scattered around the village.

How To Combat Climate Change in Ein Hod

  • Build more hemp houses
  • Focus on more efficient mazgans (air conditioners)
  • Drip irregation

Ways Ein Hodies Are Helping the Situation

  • Most people don't drive within Ein Hod
  • Many work together to maintain plants in the village
  • Wild animals roam free
